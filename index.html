Nanxy LH

aka radscal

Nanxy is currently an undergraduate Politics and Sociology student at the University of Bristol with a strong interest in social exclusion and isolation regards to intersections of disability, gender and race.

A recovering cynic, Nanxy has dreams to 'make the world a better place', and perhaps open an arts workshop café. In their spare time, they write words that too often centre on themes of belonging and nostalgia for what never was.

Nanxy has experience with magazine design and social media management and welcomes opportunities in these areas. They are also passionate about campaigning and research around health justice.
